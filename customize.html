Talk with an specialist

Step 1: Design

Share a sketch, snapshot or family heirloom with a Jewelry Expert, who will work closely with you to perfect your design and fine-tune the details. Inspiration can come from anywhere! We'll help bring your vision to life.

Step 2: Wax Model

Once you've met with a Jewelry Expert in your local store and finalized your design, our experts will create an exact wax model replica for you to examine and approve.

Step 3: Cast & Set

After you've approved the wax model of your ring, we'll cast it in the metal of your choice and set your selected diamonds or gemstones.

Step 4

Revise your recipe, finishing touches

Your ring will be polished to perfection and finished by hand before being presented to you. On average, it's about three weeks from approval until you're holding your unique design in your hands.
